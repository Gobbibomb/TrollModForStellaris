[h1][b]Overview[/b][/h1]
This mod to offer a multiplayer different experience with focus production rare resource on systems requirement phi mod.

[h1][b]How to install[/b][/h1]
Requires 1 other mods in order to function normally. Those are:

[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2247841088] Phi mp balancing[/url]

and in the launcher, the loading order should be (from top to bottom):
[olist]
[*]troll
[*]phi mp balancing
[/olist]
